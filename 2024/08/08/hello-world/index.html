    论文题目：ADAP: Adaptive & Dynamic Arc Padding for Predicting Seam Profiles in Multi-Layer-Multi-Pass Robotic Welding

    摘要：

    Incorporating robotic technology in industrial welding tasks such as the Multi Layer-Multi-Pass (MLMP) process is pivotal yet challenging, particularly in modeling and predicting the changing seam profile geometries in real time as the weldment cools. This online planning is crucial for the robotic systems to adapt their welding paths dynamically. Leveraging the recent advances in Artificial Intelligence for Science, which emphasizes large-scale deep learning for scientific innovation, this study introduces the Adaptive & Dynamic Arc Padding (ADAP) approach. ADAP employs a novel representation of weld bead profiles using imaged-based boundary and primitive arc geometries defined by arc center and radius. Utilizing datasets generated from Flow-3D simulations of the MLMP process, ADAP achieves high-accuracy, real-time seam profile predictions within 15 ms, with errors of approximately 0.73 mm in arc center position and 0.66 mm in radius.

    Keywords:

    Multi-Layer-Multi-Pass Welding, Welding Simulation, Deep Learning, Robotic Welding.
